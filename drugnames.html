about the app

Drug Names is an iOS app that helps you learn the brand and generic names for 300 popular prescription and over-the-counter drugs through multiple-choice quizzes and flashcards. Stats are tracked for completing quizzes and allow you to see which drugs and sets you are doing well with, and which ones might need extra practice.

features

  • Searchable list of all 300 drugs
  • Edit a drug's hint to fit your needs
  • Add drugs to your favorites to create a separate flash card set you can focus on
  • Customize the look of the app by changing its colors
  • Light and dark modes supported
