about me

background

I grew up in the Bay Area and went to school at UC Irvine, graduating with degrees in biology and chemistry. After receiving my Doctor of Pharmacy degree (PharmD) at the University of Minnesota College of Pharmacy, I continued to live and work in the Twin Cities for a few years. It was during this time that I started learning to code in my free time. I dabbled with Python and HTML/CSS/JS for a bit before coming across Swift.

During the pandemic, I returned to California and continued learning Swift and building small apps through online courses, articles, and YouTube videos. Programming has been a challenging, yet rewarding experience so far - serving as a creative outlet and challenging me to be a better problem-solver. I look forward to continuing this journey and hope to turn what started as a hobby into a promising career.
