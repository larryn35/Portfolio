about me

I am a self-taught iOS developer located in the San Francisco Bay Area. I started coding about a year ago, originally as a hobby. That interest continued to grow as I learned the ins and outs of the language - from variables, to loops, to classes and dictionaries. I learned how to use pandas and Plotly to analyze and visualize data and later began learning HTML and CSS so I could build websites. Learning to code has been a challenging, yet very rewarding experience thus far. From print("Hello, World!") to building websites and games, it is a great feeling to see something you envision come to life with some (or many) lines of code. I look forward to continuing this journey and hope to one day make something that would improve the lives of others. In my free time, I enjoy watching and playing sports, photography, and listening to music.
