Hello World

With this app, I wanted to practice working with a new database and creating UI/UX skills. I chose to use Firebase since I hadn’t worked with it before and it offered realtime storing and syncing of data - which is ideal for a chat app. For setting up Firebase, I followed Nathan Luu’s video on creating an iOS chat app. I expanded on his version by adding features such as storing profile pictures, recording timestamps of messages, and tracking the number of unread messages for a chatroom. I also focused on a clean design for the UI. While there is plenty of room for improvement, I was happy with the app. What I learnt: Firebase Storage, Authentication, and Cloud Firestore SDWebImageSwiftUI ImagePicker and UIViewControllerRepresentable Passing data with: @EnvironmentObject, @StateObject, and @ObservedObject
